FAQ

What is Curio?
This is a great time for writing. However as consumers we are innundated with content of varying topics and quality - much like a bad mixed tape.

Curio helps you find and consume great writing, by allowing you to 'tune content to the moment'
What's the philiosophy behind Curio?
In a choice-rich media world, what is today's equivalent of traditional TV, radio and newspaper interfaces - ones that by seamlessly bringing together choice and convenience via low cognitive-load interfaces - revolutionised media in the nineteenth and twentieth centuries?

Curio is the first step on a journey to answering this.
